My Year Abroad

Freshman year in Dublin, Ireland.

A video of exploring the roads in a new country- easily one of the most exhilirating and interesting things to do.

Freshman year of college, I went to Dublin, Ireland and studied at Trinity College. It was a crazy experience and I leanrned a ton about myself. The best part about it was being in Europe and having the ability to hop on a plane, train or boat for super cheap and go just about anywhere. I got to meet people from all over the world. I was forced out of my comfort zone when I moved from a small mountain town to a big city. Turns out, I never want to live in a city. But it was very fun to explore!
