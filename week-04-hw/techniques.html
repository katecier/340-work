Techniques

Mixing dry ingredients
Using a whisk, mix the dry ingredients gradually in a circular motion
Pure vanilla extract
Use real extract, made from the vanilla bean pod. Avoid using Imitation vanilla, which is derived from vanillin, which can be natural sourced or artificially created.
Shaping
To form the perfect cookie, loosely roll the dough with figure tips. Do not squeeze.

Did you know?

  • According to Wikipedia, The chocolate chip cookie was invented by the American chef Ruth Graves Wakefield in 1938.

    • Cookie-like hard wafers have existed for as long as baking is documented, in part because they deal with travel very well, but they were usually not sweet enough to be considered cookies by modern standards.

  • Oven temps can vary depending on the altitude at which you're baking.

  • Nestlé semi-sweet chocolate chips are a crowd favorite.

  • Wikipedia notes that the texture of a chocolate chip cookie is largely dependent on its fat composition and the type of fat used.
